Strategic Sourcing

Data Enrichment & Strategic Sourcing

When it comes to sourcing spare parts, our industrial clients all have one thing in common: they need to purchase 100% fitting parts for the best possible Total Cost of Ownership (TCO). The biggest challenge for companies such as yours, however, is to find a way to achieve this without it increasing your workload and distracting you from your core business.

Having experienced this situation ourselves, we’ve developed our Strategic Sourcing and Data Enrichment service with you in mind.

  • Cost efficient data survey services with a high-quality standard
  • Efficient sourcing with enhanced material data in conformance to industry benchmarks
  • Decades of experience
  • No additional workload
  • No need for specific training: Europower brings the know-how
  • You achieve significant cost avoidance and savings along your entire procurement supply chain
MRO Solutions

INDUSTRIAL MRO SUPPLY

MRO services involve complex life cycles of initial, emergency and regular operating spare parts. Effective MRO parts management aligns the key factors of sourcing, procurement, logistics, shipping, quality, and consolidation into one coordinated supply chain.

EUROPOWER offers industry customers a full range of options for cost-effective supply chain management of MRO parts.

MRO Supply at its Best.

We provide you with spare parts management services while constantly striving to achieve streamlined procurement costs, peak standards in quality assurance, and timely delivery of MRO parts.

Whether your operation is based in the Middle East, the Americas, or Europe, our models and contracts are tailored to your needs. We cover the entire MRO supply process, from inquiry to offer and from order to delivery, employing IT resources compatible with any ERP system.
